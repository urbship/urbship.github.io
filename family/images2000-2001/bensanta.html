Ben's Letter to Santa

Dear Santa

Merry Christmas. How are the reindeer? Are they ready for the big trip? How is Mrs. Claus? How are the elves?

I am a Beaver. I like when we sit down at the meetings because it is quiet and good.

Now it's time for the present stuff. I would like Playmobil pirate sets - 3110 Admiral, 3937 Pirate and Row Boat, and 3936 Pirate Captain. I would also like some dragon's castle Playmobil - 3888 Castle Defenders, 3317 Ghost, 3890 Baron, and 3024 Golden Knight.

I also like Lego. I would like #6437 Beach Buggy, #7312 T3-Trike, #7313 Red Planet Protector, and #7316 Excavation Searcher.

Dad asked me if I'd like a nice sweater for Christmas and I just laughed.

Have a good day at the Pole.

Love, BEN

Ben Urban Shipley
659 Farmington Ave.
Ottawa ON K1V 7H4
