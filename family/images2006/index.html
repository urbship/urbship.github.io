We went to New Hampshire for our usual one-week post-Christmas visit with the Urbans at their house on the shore of Lake Pawtuckaway in New Hampshire. We found the skating rather poor this year, so Rachel and I decided to go swimming. We went two mornings in a row - the 28th and 29th of December. These pictures were taken on the 29th by Ruth's dad, Will Urban. The next day the surface froze, so we didn't go swimming any more.
It was about 7 a.m. The air temperature was -4 C. The water was probably about freezing, because there was ice floating around in the cove.
Ooh, that's a bit chilly.
And we're in. Good thing the camera shutter goes quick - we weren't in very long!
