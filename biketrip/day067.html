Day 67: Thursday, 26 July, 1984

I didn't get into action very early today. I got up about 9:30, but I didn't actually roll out until after noon.

Headed out to the Citizen and worked my way towards the centre of town, stopping at various media on the way. I mostly got "come back tomorrow" from them. Appointments might have been better. Got one intervew with CJSB radio. I quit when I got to CHEZ. Just got tired and went home.

After supper we had this sort of reception at our house. Quiet thing, with family friends. Dad was having a disarmament meeting simultaneously. Everything quieted down after a while and I got to bed about 12:30.

Ottawa, Ont.
Trip Odometer: 7,049.6 km
