Day 99: Monday, 27 August, 1984

Slept in until about 9:30 a.m. Got up and had breakfast and slowly packed up. Rode off down the road. I called Antigonish and let them know I was coming.

Then I rode into Antigonish, to the Coady Institute. Talked to people there for a while. Their public relations person got some info from me for the local media.

I got groceries and left town. Rode along the Trans-Canada towards the Strait of Canso. This area is a little short of campgrounds, but I eventually found one after the Strait. As I was getting supper the people next to me offered their Coleman lantern and a beer. That made things a lot easier.

Stillwater to near Port Hastings, N.S.
Trip Odometer: 10,381.5 km
