Day 63: Sunday, 22 July, 1984

I scraped myself out of bed at 8:30 this morning. Was pretty slow about getting packed. Eventually got away about 10:30.

Rode through the city and arrived at Brian W.'s (3rd year Elec. Eng.) in Agincourt at exactly noon. He was having a pool party. That was rather good. It was a great day for it.

At 3 p.m. I moseyed onwards. Rode out to Whitby and found Kathy I.'s [mechanical engineering classmate] house. Went out to Harvey's with her and her family. That was pretty good. Quite a coincidence. She just happened to be passing through on her way from London, Ont., to Montreal, Que.

I rode up to a nearby campground and had my ear bent by a young question-asker as I was setting up.

Toronto to Whitby, Ont.
Trip Odometer: 6,637.7 km
