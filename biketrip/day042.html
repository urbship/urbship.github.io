Day 42: Sunday, 1 July, 1984

Got up and had a swim. Finished off the fried rice from last night. Then packed up and rode off.

Roads were pretty rough through the rest of the park. Once I got onto Hwy 1 it was pretty smooth riding, but it was also pretty busy. At least there were shoulders in most places.

Bought groceries in Kenora. Wanted a book, since I finished The Covenant. I eventually managed to buy a copy of Newsweek close to the junction of Hwy 71.

Then I turned south towards Fort Frances. Carried on about 50 km to Sioux Narrows Provincial Park. Rolled in pretty late, but I actually found a place.

Set up camp and ate supper. Lots of fireflies over the marsh that stretches out from the cliff at the back of my site.

Whiteshell Provincial Park, Man., to Sioux Narrows Provincial Park, Ont.
Trip Odometer: 4,586.3 km
