Day 15: Monday, 4 June, 1984

Got off to a late start today. Rode part way up the pass and then stopped at a viewpoint. There, eating lunch, were the people I met at Long Beach a couple of weeks ago! So I had lunch with them.

Continued on up the pass, taking pictures (and my time). A train went through the spiral tunnels when I was there.

Crossed the Great Divide and rode down to Lake Louise. From there I headed north.

Had a flat, so I was really late getting to Mosquito Creek. Decided to stay in the Youth Hostel here. It's only $4.50 a night. I'm rather pooped now.

Golden, B.C. to Mosquito Creek, Alta.
Trip Odometer: 1,587.3 km
