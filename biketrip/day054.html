Day 54: Friday, 13 July, 1984

Got up and cooked breakfast. Had an audience, of course. The birthday boy returned to life and watched me pack up, etc.

Got going about ten and rode pretty steadily down Hwy 6. Didn't stop much until I got to Fergus. Then bought groceries and took off down the back roads through Elora, Elmira, St. Jacobs, etc., till I got to Phillipsburg.

Found the FASS [stands for Faculty, Administration, Staff, and Students--it's a campus theatre group at the University of Waterloo] campout. Set up and cooked supper. It felt really good to face the prospect of not going anywhere tomorrow.

Lots of interesting conversations with FASSists around the fire. Brad T. brought a telescope out, too.

Durham to Phillipsburg, Ont.
Trip Odometer: 6,359.6 km
