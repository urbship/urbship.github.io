Day 25: Thursday, 14 June, 1984

I slept in a tad today. Got up, had a bath, and then breakfast. Then I got packed and went out to meet the press.

I talked to the North Battleford New Optimist and the Battleford Telegraph. That made me so late leaving that I didn't get on the road till 11:45. So I had to pedal like mad. Just stopped a couple of times for quick snacks.

Met Gary B. [friend in Saskatoon] at Borden Bridge. That was a boost. He rode in with me, the CFQC-TV camera crew pacing us for a while.

Rodney C. [another Saskatoon friend] met us and then after a while we met the Saskatoon Cycling Club, who escorted me to Knox Church.

There was a nice reception, an interview by CKOM Radio and a TV10 (PBS) interview. Then supper in a restaurant at CUSO expense. Then to Blair P.'s to crash.

North Battleford to Saskatoon, Sask.
Trip Odometer: 3,013.4 km
