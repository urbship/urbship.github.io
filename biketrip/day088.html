Day 88: Thursday, 16 August, 1984

Got up and had breakfast, etc., and took off. I rode towards Truro. There was a bit of a hump to climb over between there. I eventually got to go downhill for a while, though. The wind was also against me. The weatherman says the wind will change tomorrow.

I rode into Truro and found the newspaper by accident. After talking to them I went to the radio station. They didn't have time. I bought food and took off.

I rode along on some nice small roads. Got dumped on by a heavy rain for a while. I couldn't find the campground that was on the map, so I set up by the road.

Lyons Brook to near Densmore Mills, N.S.
Trip Odometer: 9,149.6 km
