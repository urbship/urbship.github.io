Day 96: Friday, 24 August, 1984

Got up around 9 a.m. and got going on a few things, like breakfast, laundry, etc. I got the laundry mostly done by about 10:45.

Then we went down to the main newspaper office and I had an interview with a rather cute journalist. Then dropped by the CUSO office and read all Dad's notes and phoned Annapolis Valley Radio.

After lunch, went looking for an antenna for my radio--failed. Then got a few bearings at the Trail Shop. Had a long talk with a guy there.

Back to CUSO. Went to the Grad Club with Linda and met Nancy E., Phil S., and various other people [all CUSOBOTs]. Jim H. [another CUSOBOT] is in town but wasn't there. Then out to supper with Linda and her neato boyfriend and back here to watch Dallas.

Halifax, N.S.
Trip Odometer: 10,047.7 km
