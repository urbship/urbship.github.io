Day 19: Friday, 8 June, 1984

Well, it wasn't raining when I got up this morning. I had a shower and read for a while. Eventually packed up and left.

Made some calls from Entwistle, to Edmonton and Saskatoon. Then I rode on. Had a wind against me almost all the way.

It seemed like a long day, even though it was much shorter than yesterday. Towards the end of the day, I got spits of rain, but not enough to make me put my poncho on.

I made it through the city and got to the right place. Had a meal and a visit with the Ws [my aunt and uncle and cousins]. Uncle Bob has pneumonia, so he wasn't very much in evidence. Hope he gets better soon.

I've averaged 137 km/day since Vancouver.

Near Evansburg to Edmonton, Alta.
Trip Odometer: 2,181.4 km
