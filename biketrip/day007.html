Day 7: Sunday, 27 May, 1984

Got up late and had breakfast with Sue C. and then rushed off to the ferry. I missed the 9 a.m. by four minutes. Got the 11 o'clock, after a good talk with the cashiers in the terminal restaurant!

The ferry ride was good. 20 km to Erik K.'s house. Had a rather enjoyable afternoon with him and his roommate. Went up to the Capillano swinging bridge and then to the beach below University of British Columbia.

Then in the evening, believe it or not, we went to a Thomas Dolby concert. That was great! And now I'm watching a dumb movie and thinking about going to sleep.

Nanaimo to North Vancouver, B.C.
Trip Odometer: 583.9 km
