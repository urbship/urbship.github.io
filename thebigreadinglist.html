The Big Reading List

This is an interesting list of great works that I pulled from the sources at the bottom. Guess I'll dip into it over a long period, but not as diligently as I've read the other lists. It's kind of like the life list a birder would have, but for books instead. I've checked off the ones I've already read. Books that I am in the process of reading are marked with an open book.
