Norwegian Book Club Top 100 List

Bokklubben World Library is a list of classical books, mostly novels, that was compiled by the Norwegian Book Club in 2002. They asked one hundred writers from fifty-four different countries to each suggest 10 books, from which they selected the top 100. They tried to reflect world literature, with books from around the world and from different cultures and time periods. The books are not ranked, but the organizers regard Don Quixote as the "best literary work ever written."
